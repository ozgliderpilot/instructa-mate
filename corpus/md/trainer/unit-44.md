# Unit 44 — Glider Pilot Certificate (Application Authorised)

<!-- page: 44-1 -->

## AIM
<!-- content_type: aim -->

To ensure that the pilot has completed all the pre-requisites for the application for a GFA Glider Pilot Certificate.

## PRE-REQUISITE UNITS
<!-- content_type: admin -->

- GPC Units 1 to 43

## COMPLEMENTARY UNITS
<!-- content_type: admin -->

There are no complementary units to this training.

## COMPETENCY ELEMENTS AND PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
<!-- content_type: competency -->

### 1. Completion of all pre- requisite GPC Elements. • Demonstrate

  - Completion of all previous GPC elements by examination of the student’s training record.

## KEY MESSAGES
<!-- content_type: key_messages -->

- The award of a GPC recognises that the student has been trained and assessed as competent to safely operate a sailplane as an independently proficient GFA soaring pilot following satisfactory completion of the GPC Training Syllabus.

## LESSON PLANNING AND CONDUCT
<!-- content_type: briefing -->

### Briefing

### Completion of GPC Application

The student needs to complete the first section of the GPC Application Form (F007).

### CFI Certification

The CFI needs to ensure that the student has completed the GPC training syllabus and that the Club holds the associated training records.

Ensure the student logbook is endorsed IAW MOSP 2 (10.6).

<!-- page: 44-2 -->

### Credential Submission

Once the form is certified the student must upload a copy of the form as a credential in Go Membership.

## FLIGHT EXERCISES
<!-- content_type: exercise -->

There are no flight exercises associated with this unit.

## COMMON PROBLEMS
<!-- content_type: common_problems -->

### Incomplete training records.

Student may not have had instructor sign off on all elements.

## THREAT AND ERROR MANAGEMENT
<!-- content_type: airmanship -->

- Ensure pilot has an up-to-date logbook and training record.
- Ensure that all pilot details are accurate on the form.

## TRAINING MATERIALS AND REFERENCES
<!-- content_type: admin -->

- MOSP 2, Section 10.6.
- GFA Unit 44 Pilot Guide.
